Self-Funding DeFi Ecosystem on Polygon

What is DIGGER_CRYPTO

DIGGER_CRYPTO is a decentralized financial ecosystem built on Polygon. It is designed to be self-sustaining, deflationary and transparent. The ecosystem operates through real on-chain mechanics that generate liquidity, reduce supply and maintain long-term stability.

PARASITA Protocol

PARASITA is the deflationary core of the ecosystem. Each transaction applies a small tribute that funds the protocol and permanently removes tokens from circulation.

  • Deflationary burn mechanism
  • Self-funded treasury model
  • No reliance on external capital

MAMACOCO Utility Token

MAMACOCO acts as the liquidity and utility bridge of the ecosystem. It supports trading stability and future staking or vault mechanics.

  • Liquidity bridge
  • Utility expansion token
  • Ecosystem stabilization

APE-LP Liquidity Pool

APE-LP represents the liquidity foundation between PARASITA and MAMACOCO. Strong LP mechanics reduce volatility and enable sustainable price discovery.

  • Liquidity backbone
  • Price stability
  • Efficient on-chain swaps
