PARASITA — Tribute Mechanic (Technical)

PARASITA applies a 3.0% transaction fee: 1.5% to Treasury (ownerWallet) and 1.5% burned. Treasury funds are used for automated market-making and buyback operations to stabilize price and add liquidity.
