Ecosystem Overview

DIGGER_CRYPTO is a decentralized ecosystem built on the Polygon blockchain. The architecture is designed to operate without external funding, venture capital or artificial price support. Each component serves a distinct on-chain function while reinforcing system stability.

Parasita Protocol — Smart Contract Logic

The Parasita Protocol is a deflationary ERC-20 smart contract that implements controlled supply reduction through transaction-level mechanics. The contract logic is simple, transparent and intentionally minimal.

  • Standard ERC-20 transfer logic
  • Internal fee mechanism routed to protocol-controlled addresses
  • Permanent burn reducing total circulating supply
  • No mint functions after deployment
  • No hidden owner minting privileges

Parasita is designed to behave as a *value-extraction protocol*: value is accumulated through transaction flow rather than speculative minting.

Mamacoco Token — Utility & Stability Layer

Mamacoco operates as a utility and liquidity-supporting asset inside the DIGGER_CRYPTO ecosystem. Its smart contract follows a conventional ERC-20 model to ensure maximum compatibility with DeFi tools and liquidity platforms.

  • Utility-focused token without excessive taxation
  • Used for liquidity pairing and ecosystem expansion
  • Predictable supply behavior
  • Optimized for low-cost Polygon transactions

Mamacoco acts as an operational asset rather than a speculative instrument, allowing the ecosystem to grow organically.

APE LP — Liquidity Infrastructure

APE LP represents the liquidity pool between Parasita and Mamacoco. Liquidity pools are the backbone of decentralized price discovery.

  • Automated Market Maker (AMM) based pricing
  • Reduces volatility through deep liquidity
  • Supports sustainable transaction flow
  • Non-custodial and permissionless

The LP is intentionally left market-driven to avoid artificial price control.
